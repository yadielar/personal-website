About me

Hi, my name is Yadiel Arroyo and I'm a designer and developer.

Specifically, I design and develop websites and user interfaces. Since I was a kid I've loved both drawing, and reading science books. Eventually, when it was time to choose a career, I had to decide which one I loved the most. Should I become a graphic designer? Or maybe an engineer? I finally decided to become a graphic designer because I loved the creative aspect.

But as time passed, my love for science and technology only grew stronger. After taking some web design classes in college, it occurred to me that I could join my two loves. So I became an interface designer and developer, and I've been loving the ride ever since!

Hello 2 Worlds!

I give you the best of both worlds without sacrificing quality. Though design is where it all started, I’ve grown to love code too. I appreciate a well designed product as much as good, clean and efficient code.

My expertise lies within HTML, CSS & JavaScript. I make sure that the final product follows the original design as closely as possible, and if unexpected problems arise during development, I have the necessary design skills to make decisions as needed.
